# Pipeline

AgentFOS turns agent intent into an auditable allocation.

```text
Agent
↓
Policy Skill
↓
Opportunity Skill
↓
Risk Skill
↓
Allocation Skill
↓
Optional Pharos Proof
```

## Policy Skill

Checks whether the requested action is allowed under capital and risk constraints.

## Opportunity Skill

Fetches RWA opportunity data, including APY, TVL, and benchmark spreads.

## Risk Skill

Scores protocols with Risk Engine v2. The score is deterministic and includes a breakdown.

## Allocation Skill

Ranks eligible assets and generates allocation percentages, dollar amounts, portfolio APY, average risk, and narrative.

## Optional Pharos Proof

When configured, AgentFOS writes the top allocation on-chain with protocol, risk score, APY basis points, caller, and timestamp.
